Brooklyn & New York City Life

While Manhattan certainly has it's own allure, I never felt completely at home there, preferring instead the open skies and leafy streets that make up much of Brooklyn. I took the leap in 2006, witnessing an era that was punctuated by the rise of hipster culture which over the past decade has spread far and wide, and never looked back.

What you'll find here are often rambling thoughts and reports about what's going on in my favourite borough (and my second one too), from culture and food to shopping and real estate.
